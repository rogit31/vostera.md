General Toblerone, otherwise known as Toby, is a dwarf general who is a member of the Ashirian Army. He was the general to take [[Hemlock]] under his wing after the fire occurred in his hometown of [[Nagan]]. He has a deep cracking voice and currently works as a drill officer.
