The whispering grove is a large temperate forest in the center of [[Kyrenia]]. It has small communities spread throughout it, hidden under the covers of illusory magic, as an ancient defense mechanism in the earlier ethnic wars against the [[Losdraeg Kingdom]]. 

Cheetoh's Angels are currently in the whispering grove doing some research outside of the grasp of the ashir empire, uncovering some hidden and secretive truths, led by [[Kynen Ennore]] to the [[Whispering Grove Library]].
